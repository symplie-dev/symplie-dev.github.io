Emotie

A Google Chrome extension for quickly adding ASCII emoticons and kaomojis to your text

Select, Click, Copy!

Easily select a kaomoji from your list, copy it to your clipboard and paste it to your text

Personalize for your needs

Create, edit, and delete your most frequently used emoticons and kaomogis

Create

Emotie allows you to create and save your favorite custom-made kaomojis to your browser.

Edit

Tag and name your kaomojis with meaningful descriptions to better organize and find your masterpieces.

Share

Awe your friends with speed and ease as you casually drop emoties in your conversations.
